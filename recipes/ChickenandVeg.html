Roasted Chicken and Veggies

Description

Yields: 4 servings

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 40-50 minutes

This recipe is fantastic for a weeknight meal because everything cooks together on a single sheet pan. The chicken stays juicy, and the vegetables get wonderfully tender and caramelized.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 - 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into 1.5-inch pieces
  • 1 lb small potatoes
  • 1 lb broccoli florets, cut into bite size pieces
  • 2 carrots, peeled and cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into 1-inch wedges
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large lemon, half thinly sliced (for placing on chicken), half juiced (for dressing)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat and Prep: Preheat your oven to 400째F (200째C). Line a large rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper for easier cleanup, if desired.
  2. Combine Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the quartered potatoes, broccoli florets, carrot chunks, and red onion wedges.
  3. Season Everything: Drizzle the vegetables with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, and season generously with salt and pepper. Toss to coat evenly.
  4. Season Chicken: Add the chicken pieces to the same bowl (or a separate one if you prefer). Drizzle with the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Add the minced garlic, dried Italian seasoning, paprika (if using), and a good pinch of salt and pepper. Squeeze the juice from half of the lemon over the chicken and toss to coat.
  5. Arrange on Pan: Spread the seasoned vegetables in an even layer on the prepared baking sheet. Place the seasoned chicken pieces among the vegetables, ensuring they are in a single layer. Arrange the thin lemon slices over the chicken pieces.
  6. Roast: Bake for 40-50 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through (internal temperature of 165째F / 74째C) and the vegetables are tender and slightly caramelized. If you're using bone-in chicken, it might take a bit longer. For extra crispiness, you can broil for the last 2-3 minutes, but watch it carefully to prevent burning.
  7. Serve: Remove from the oven. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley if you like. Serve hot directly from the pan!
